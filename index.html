About me

Greetings! I am a third year Physics PhD student at University of Otago, where I am supervised by Prof. Craig J. Rodger.

With the support of Prof. Craig J. Rodger, my research has also benefited greatly from the guidance of Dr. Mark Clilverd and Dr. Daniel Mac Manus. As an international student living in New Zealand, I have also received generous support from Dr. Lisa Evans.

My current research focuses on Space Weather, reactive power and harmonics in the New Zealand electrical network. If you are also interested in this topic, please feel free to reach out by email.

Current Research Focuses

(1) Space Weather and geomagnetically induced currents.
(2) Reactive power impacts in the New Zealand electrical network.
(3) Transformer harmonics and power-system response during geomagnetic storms.
